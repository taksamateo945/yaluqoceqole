The Truth AboutChanning Tatums Family, Magic Mike, His Love Life

Channing Tatum is one of the most popular and handsome actors in Hollywood.

He is known for his roles in movies like Magic Mike, 21 Jump Street, and The Vow.

But did you know that he has a sister who is also in the entertainment industry?

And that his movie Magic Mike is partly inspired by his own experience as a stripper.

And that he is currently dating a famous actress?

In this article, we will reveal the truth about Channing Tatum’s sister, his career, and his love life.

Who is Channing Tatum’s sister?

Channing Tatum‘s sister is Paige Tatum, who is a model and actress.

She is the younger sister of Channing and has largely stayed out of the public eye.

She was born on November 5, 1984, in Cullman, Alabama, to Kay Tatum (née Faust), an airline worker, and Glenn Tatum, a construction worker.

Paige has appeared in some movies and TV shows, such as The Trap (2005), The Witches of Oz (2011), and The Walking Dead (2012).

She also worked as a producer for the documentary Earth Made of Glass (2010), which was executive-produced by Channing.

Paige and Channing are close and often support each other’s work.

Paige attended the premiere of Magic Mike XXL (2015) with her brother and posted a photo on Instagram with the caption: \”So proud of my big brother!\”

Is Magic Mike based on a true story?

No, Magic Mike is not based on a true story, but it is partially inspired by true events.

In fact, the movie takes inspiration from the life of its lead actor, Channing Tatum, who played the role of Mike.

Back when Tatum was eighteen before he became a star, he worked as a stripper to make a living.

He went by the name of Chan Crawford and performed at a local nightclub in Tampa, Florida.

Mike later confessed that he wasn’t particularly proud of it, but he wasn’t ashamed of it either.

He wanted to make a movie about his experiences as a stripper and share the energy and atmosphere of that world.

However, except for some superficial details from his life, the story is mainly fictional.

He clarified that the movie is based on the world that he had once been a part of, but there is \”not one character that I took from my real life\”.

How old is Channing Tatum now?

Channing Tatum was born on April 26, 1980, in Cullman, Alabama.

 Is Channing Tatum married with children?

Channing Tatum was married to Jenna Dewan from 2009 to 2019.

They met on the set of the movie Step Up (2006) and fell in love.

The two got engaged in September 2008 and tied the knot in July 2009 in Malibu, California.

They welcomed their daughter Everly in May 2013 in London, England.

However, their marriage did not last and they announced their separation in April 2018.

They officially divorced in November 2019 and agreed to co-parent their daughter amicably.

Since then, both Tatum and Dewan have moved on with new partners.

Dewan got engaged to actor Steve Kazee in February 2020 and gave birth to their son Callum in March 2020.

Tatum started dating singer Jessie J in October 2018 but they broke up in December 2019.

He then began dating actress Zoë Kravitz in August 2021 after meeting her on the set of their movie Pussy Island (2022), which she directed and he starred in.

They have been spotted together several times in New York City and Los Angeles, looking cozy and happy.
