Jillian Michaels Marries Fashion Designer DeShanna Marie Minuto In Africa

Jillian Michaels and DeShanna Marie Minuto are married!

The couple, who announced their engagement in November of 2021, just revealed that they said their “I Dos” and tied the knot.

Click inside to read all the details…

Jillian shared with fans that they said their vows during a private ceremony in Namibia, posting a few pictures and video from the event on her Instagram.

“‘Just married’ @deshannamarie Michaels – it’s an honor and an adventure saying I do to you,” Jillian first wrote.

She went on, “Finding you… my person… and eloping in Africa together has been one of the most magical and transformative chapters of my life.”

Jillian also shared a special “thank you to Susan Neva at @alluringafrica , the team at @wearewilderness and the Himba people for helping us commemorate our union in style. We will treasure these experiences for the rest of our lives.”

The couple are expected to hold a bigger celebration with family and friends in Venice next year.

Jillian was previously engaged to Heidi Rhoades. They share two children.
