About

Eslabon Armado is a Mexican musical group consisting of Pedro Tovar on vocals, Gabriel Hidalgo on guitar, and Brian Tovar on bass. The group was formed in 2017, and gained widespread popularity in 2020 with the release of their album “Vibras de Noche”.

Their music is primarily in the regional Mexican genre, but also incorporates elements of trap and hip-hop. Eslabon Armado’s lyrics often focus on themes of love, heartbreak, and relationships.

The group has been nominated for several awards in Mexico, including the Premios Juventud and the Latin American Music Awards.
