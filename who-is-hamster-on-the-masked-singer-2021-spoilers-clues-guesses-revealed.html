Who is Hamster on The Masked Singer 2021? Spoilers, Clues &amp; Guesses Revealed!

Hamster just made his debut during a wild card slot for Group A of The Masked Singer!

The masked celeb performed during night two of the season six premiere on Thursday (September 23).

Hamster joined the remaining Group A contestants – Skunk, Bull, and Pufferfish – as well as the new wild card contestant Baby.

Click inside to check out all of the clues and guesses for Hamster…

FIRST PERFORMANCE CLUES:

– In the clue package, Hamster said, “I wanted to become the Hamster because my body is unfortunately shaped a lot like a hamster.”

– The hamster is wearing headphones.

– Says he is always dropping from one project to another with his famous friends.

– A poster for Alcatraz Island was shown.

– The hamster said he is always in the arena.

– The hamster’s locker was revealed and he said it’s “sunny” and he “likes to play basebell.” When Jenny McCarthy said this reminded her of Danny DeVito, he said he’s a “lot taller.”

– He performed the song “Pretty Woman” and you can watch the performance below!

PANEL GUESSES: Bill Murray, Brendan Fraser, Albert Brooks, Tim Allen, Kelsey Grammer, Andy Richter, Jack Black
