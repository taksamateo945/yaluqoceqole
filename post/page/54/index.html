Shortest Person In The World: From Short Stature to Guinness Glory

published on April 29, 2024
Afshin Esmaeil Ghaderzadeh, a 21-year-old Iranian man, holds the title of the shortest person in the world. He measures 65.24 centimeters (2 feet 1.6 inches) in height. Guinness World Records officially recognized him as the shortest living man on December 20, 2022. Afshin’s short stature is a result of a rare type of dwarfism known as primordial dwarfism, which hampers both prenatal and postnatal growth.
