Gwen Stefani, Gavin Rossdale Reunite After Split: Details

Call it the friend zone. Gwen Stefani and Gavin Rossdale reunited on Saturday, Aug. 15, at the Balboa Fun Zone in Newport Beach, Calif., for a family outing following their shocking divorce announcement two weeks ago.

A source tells Us Weekly that the estranged spouses were together with their three sons at the beachside amusement park. For the outing, Bush rocker Rossdale wore jean shorts, a white T-shirt, high-knee socks, and a bucket hat, while Stefani dressed in loose-fitting black attire with a red baseball cap.

While their middle son 6-year-old Zuma, tried out one particular attraction, the proud parents both took photos from their iPhones. Eldest brother Kingston, 9, patiently watched his younger sibling from the side. (The No Doubt songstress and Bush frontman also share a baby son Apollo, 17 months, although it remains unclear whether he was with the family on Sunday.)

Stefani, 45, and Rossdale, 49, confirmed to Us Weekly on Aug. 3, that they were splitting after 13 years of marriage. “While the two of us have come to the mutual decision that we will no longer be partners in marriage, we remain partners in parenthood and are committed to jointly raising our three sons in a happy and healthy environment,” they told Us. “To that end, we respectfully request privacy from the media during this time.”

A source subsequently told Us that the parents of three were focusing on their kids. “Of course Gwen is upset. She really gave it her all and tried to make it work as hard as she could for years,” one insider told Us at the time. “She was in love with him and they had the family she always dreamed of.”

Since their split announcement, Stefani has returned to work at The Voice, where she joins her fellow coach, Blake Shelton, who is also coping with life after his recent divorce from Miranda Lambert. While Shelton “seems to have moved on from Miranda and is just over it,” an observer at a taping for the show last week told Us that the No Doubt frontwoman “seems sad.”
