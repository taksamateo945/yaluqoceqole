Shia LaBeouf's Heated Fight With Girlfriend Mia Goth Caught on Video

Shia LaBeouf got into a heated argument with girlfriend Mia Goth in Germany, and it was all caught on tape. In the video, released by Entertainment Tonight on Monday, July 27, the Transformers star, 29, says he “would’ve killed her” while locals drove him away from Goth, 22.

“I don’t want to touch a woman, I don’t want to hit a woman, but I’m being pushed,” LaBeouf is heard telling a group of men who later drive him to the airport following his confrontation with his longtime girlfriend. 

Before driving off, LaBeouf confronts Goth in the street and tells her: “I don’t want to touch you. I don’t want to be aggressive. This is the kind of s—t that makes a person abusive.”

LaBeouf later tells the men in the car: “If I’d have stayed there, I would have killed her.”
